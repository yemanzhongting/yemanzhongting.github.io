My name is Yan Zhang, born in 1997 and from Wuhan University, China. I received my undergraduate and master’s degrees from school of geodesy and geomatics, Wuhan University. Now I’m currently pursuing my GIS PhD degree at the State Key Laboratory of Remote Sensing Information Engineering for Surveying and Mapping,supervised by Prof. Nengcheng Chen in Geospatial Sensor Web Group. Recently, I am visiting student at Urban analysis lab of National University of Singapore advised by Dr. Filip Biljecki. My research interesting including natural language processing&computer vision in geo-tagged text&image, webgis and geospatial knowledge mining. Link
